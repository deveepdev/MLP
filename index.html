Marie Lyne Phare Photographe Animalière

What really catches my eye

the way they look at me to say things they can't say out loud! What really warms my heart: answering this call and snapping a photo of it! Photography lets me get into the animal's world. I get to see what they're doing in the moment and that's it. I blend in with their surroundings until they notice me. Interactions happen and communication starts flowing. The camera I'm holding captures all the different angles of these interactions. It's so exciting to have challenges coming at me every second! The biggest challenge is this three-way relationship. That is, the animal, me, and this black thing that talks with 'clicks' and can sometimes mess up the relationship we've established. If challenges are what help me grow and develop, this one teaches me how to build and keep a strong relationship with my subject no matter what. Being able to get into an animal's personal life is such a blessing! In the end, we're not so different from them. They love being around living things that respect and love them, and that brings them joy. It's a celebration of the beautiful interspecies relationships we have, and that's the foundation of my approach and why I want to share it.
